What We Do?

Premium Washing Services

Exterior Washing

Exterior washing is a thorough cleaning of a vehicle's outer surfaces, including washing, scrubbing, and rinsing the body, windows, mirrors, and tires. It removes dirt, grime, road salt, and other contaminants, leaving the vehicle looking fresh and well-maintained.

Interior Washing

Interior washing involves cleaning the inside of a vehicle, including vacuuming carpets, seats, and floor mats, wiping down surfaces like the dashboard, doors, and center console, and sanitizing areas like cup holders and air vents. It also includes cleaning windows and mirrors for a spotless, fresh interior

Vacuum Cleaning

Vacuum cleaning involves using a vacuum cleaner to remove dirt, dust, and debris from various surfaces such as carpets, upholstery, and floor mats. It effectively cleans hard-to-reach areas like under seats and in crevices, ensuring a dust-free and tidy environment

Seats Washing

Seat washing involves deep cleaning the fabric, leather, or upholstery of a vehicle's seats to remove stains, dirt, and odors. This process may include vacuuming, scrubbing with specialized cleaning agents, and sometimes steam cleaning, leaving the seats fresh, sanitized, and looking like new.

Window Wiping

Window wiping involves cleaning the interior and exterior surfaces of a vehicle's windows and mirrors using a cloth or squeegee and a cleaning solution. This process removes dirt, smudges, and streaks, ensuring clear visibility and a spotless, polished appearance

Wet Cleaning

Wet cleaning involves using water and specialized cleaning solutions to wash surfaces. In the context of vehicle care, it includes wiping down or scrubbing areas like seats, carpets, and dashboards with a damp cloth or sponge to remove stains, grime, and spills, leaving the surfaces clean and refreshed.

Oil Changing

Oil changing is the process of draining old engine oil from a vehicle and replacing it with fresh oil, along with a new oil filter. This maintenance task helps lubricate engine parts, reduce friction, and ensure the engine runs smoothly, enhancing performance and extending the life of the vehicle.

Brake Reparing

Brake repairing involves diagnosing and fixing issues with a vehicle's braking system, such as replacing worn brake pads, resurfacing or replacing brake rotors, and inspecting or adjusting brake fluid levels. Proper brake maintenance ensures safe stopping power, enhances vehicle safety, and prolongs the lifespan of the brake components.
